Hi and welcome to my site! 😄

I have experience leading and scaling data and engineering teams at companies whose products impact millions of people. I strive to build high levels of trust within my teams, and to empower those around me to do their best work by promoting extreme ownership, curiosity, transparency, and a strong writing culture.

I currently support [Drop](https://www.joindrop.com)'s engineering and data teams, where we're on a mission to empower people financially.

I previously co-founded and served as CTO of Canopy Labs, which we exited to Drop in 2018. Among others, companies like Expedia, JetBlue, BMO, Roots, Indigo, and the Seattle Seahawks used our product to create highly personalized experiences for their customers through marketing campaigns. We grew the company to 35 people and millions of $ per year in revenue.

In what now seems like a lifetime ago, I completed a PhD in theoretical physics, doing research in integrability in gauge and string theories at the [Perimeter Institute](https://perimeterinstitute.ca/) and the [University of Waterloo](https://uwaterloo.ca/).

I'm originally from Peru, and now live in Canada with my wife and two kids. I enjoy practicing Brazilian jiu-jitsu, having a good espresso, and consider myself a bit of a melomaniac.

If you think I can be helpful, don't hesitate to reach out at hi@jorgeescobedo.com.
